# TRAITS (Passive Abilities)

## SOVEREIGN TRAITS (Class-Granted)

### ‚ö° Storm Immunity
**Type:** Defensive / Passive
**Description:** Lia is completely immune to all forms of electrical and lightning damage. This includes natural lightning, magical lightning attacks, static discharge, and any electricity-based effects. **Effect:** 100% immunity to Lightning/Electrical damage. Cannot be stunned, paralyzed, or affected by lightning-based status effects.

**Type:** Resource / Passive  
**Description:** Vex invested heavily in Lia's magical potential. Her mana pool is not merely large ‚Äî it's a reservoir that dwarfs archmages who've studied for centuries. Where others cast with cups, she casts with oceans.
**Effect:** +1000% Maximum Mana. Base MP 120 ‚Üí 1,200 MP at Level 30.

**Type:** Resource / Passive

**Type:** Offensive / Passive
**Description:** When Lia casts, the magic responds to her intensity. Her spells don't just work ‚Äî they SURGE. Every bolt strikes harder, every wind cuts deeper, every storm rages fiercer.
**Effect:** +100% Spell Damage/Effect potency.

### ‚ö° Sovereign's Vitality
**Type:** Regeneration / Passive
**Description:** Lia's body regenerates at an impossible rate, as if the storm itself feeds her life force. Wounds close in minutes. Exhaustion fades in moments. She recovers from near-death faster than most recover from a papercut.
**Effect:** +1000% HP/MP/SP Regeneration rates.

### ‚ö° Storm Flight
**Type:** Movement / Passive
**Description:** The winds obey Lia as naturally as her own limbs. She can ride air currents, levitate on pressure differentials, and soar through the sky with perfect control. Not a spell ‚Äî an extension of her being.

**Description:** Lia's presence affects the weather around her. Her emotions bleed into the atmosphere ‚Äî anger brings thunderheads, joy brings warm breezes, arousal brings humid tension. She can consciously suppress this, but her natural state is to be one with the sky.
**Effect:** Passive weather influence in 1-mile radius. Can consciously control weather patterns. Emotional states cause involuntary weather shifts.

### ‚ö° Divine Investment
**Type:** Special / Passive
**Description:** Vex has marked Lia as a Favorite ‚Äî one of seven currently alive. This grants her direct communication with the System, unique quest access, and a degree of protection that even gods must acknowledge.
**Effect:** Direct Vex communication. Unique quest access. System-granted protection from certain fates. Accelerated growth potential.
